Ahmedabad Municipal Transport Service (AMTS)

The Ahmedabad Municipal Transport Service (AMTS) is the lifeline of Ahmedabad's public transport system. Operated by the Ahmedabad Municipal Corporation (AMC), AMTS serves as an essential mode of transport for daily commuters, students, and office-goers.

Our Mission

AMTS is committed to providing safe, affordable, and efficient public transportation to the people of Ahmedabad. Our focus is on continuous improvement, technological advancements, and enhanced passenger convenience.
