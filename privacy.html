Privacy Policy

Introduction:
Welcome to **MY AMTS**, the official app for Ahmedabad's **Ahmedabad Municipal Transport Service (AMTS)**. This app provides real-time bus tracking, digital ticketing, and other services to improve your travel experience.

MY AMTS is developed and maintained by **Ahmedabad Municipal Transport Service (AMTS)**.

1. Information Collection and Use

To provide a seamless experience, we collect the following data:

  • **IP Address** - To identify and secure network connections.
  • **User Activity Data** - Pages visited, time spent, and interactions for analytics.
  • **Device Information** - Operating system, app version for compatibility and debugging.
  • **Geolocation Data** - To provide location-based services such as nearby bus stops.

2. Third-Party Services

We use third-party tools for analytics, advertisements, and service improvements:

  • **Google Play Services**
  • **AdMob** (for ads and monetization)
  • **Google Analytics for Firebase** (to analyze user behavior)
  • **Firebase Crashlytics** (to monitor and fix app crashes)
  • **Facebook SDK** (for social media integration)

3. Opt-Out Rights

You can stop data collection by uninstalling the app. Some services allow you to disable tracking through your device settings.

4. Data Retention Policy

We retain user data only as long as necessary to provide services. If you stop using the app, we delete your data after a reasonable period.

5. Security Measures

Your security is important to us. We implement encryption, access controls, and secure data storage to protect your information.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Any changes will be posted here, and we encourage users to review it regularly.
